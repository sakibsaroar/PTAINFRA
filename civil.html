CIVIL CONSTRUCTION


PTA Steel and Civil Construction undertake a wide range of sub-structure foundation projects such as earth excavation, pile foundation, and RCC casting for various concrete or composite structures.

Our expertise covers across diverse sectors including power generation, transmission and distribution, water treatment plants, dam & embankments, renewable energy sector, railway platform and many more.
