FIRE SAFETY & CONTROLS


PTA Fire Safety & Controls, a division of PTA Infrastructure Limited, encompasses a broad spectrum of engineering, design, consultation, supply, erection and construction in Commercial Facilities, Residential Building, Textile & Garments, Oil & Gas sectors, Airports & Aviation, Health care & Education, Military & Police, Power Generation, Renewable Energy Sector etc. At PTA Fire Safety & Controls, we are dedicated to ensuring the highest standards in fire safety and control measures, contributing to the overall resilience and reliability of infrastructure projects.

Goals


Our central objective is to establish and maintain a robust and adaptive fire safety framework that ensures the highest level of protection for lives, property, and the environment. Through innovative solutions and proactive measures, our core goal is to enhance fire safety resilience across diverse sectors

Safety


By prioritizing prevention, preparedness, and rapid response, we strive to create a safer and more secure environment, contributing to the well-being of communities and the sustainability of infrastructure projects. Our all systems & equipment are in compliance with the guidelines of NFPA, UL, Global Mark, FM, Bangladesh Fire Service & Civil Defense, BNBC and BSTI.
